ABSTRACT 
Stock market prediction, anomaly detection, and risk analysis are essential for 
informed investment decisions in the banking sector, particularly with rapid 
technological advancements enabling accurate data analysis. This study applies the 
Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) algorithm to analyze stock price 
movements of major Indian banks, including State Bank of India (SBI), HDFC 
Bank, and ICICI Bank. Stock price fluctuations are examined, and anomaly 
detection identifies unusual price movements. Based on these anomalies, risk 
analysis helps investors evaluate bank stability and risk. Furthermore, LSTM-
based stock price prediction forecasts future prices using historical trends, 
enabling investors to anticipate market movements and make proactive, informed, 
and potentially profitable investment decisions.
